About Me

My name is Teagan, a programmer who failed and kept trying. During my last year of high school, I thought, "Why should I wait to create?" So I researched programming fields, I found many I liked, and some I never wanted to do. The final decision was to pursue web development, so I made this blog.


I aim to provide my experience and thoughts on how to turn a passion into a career. And by viewing this blog I hope you will advance earlier than I did.
