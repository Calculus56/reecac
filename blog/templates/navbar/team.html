About The Website

We aim to provide our experience and thoughts on how to turn a passion into a career, and provide a place for experienced and inexperienced coders alike to share their goals and project ideas with the community. And by viewing this blog I hope you will advance earlier than we did.

REECAC is an acronym. It stands for Read, Entertain & Exhibit Coding Achievements with your Community.
